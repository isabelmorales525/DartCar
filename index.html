DART BALLOON CAR RACE

Creators: Izzy and Juliette

Part 1: The Balloon Game

    This is a partner game, so you will need someone to play with you on this computer.
    The goal of the game is to pop the balloons by pressing the space bar on your keyboards.
    You will have have three tries to pop the balloons before your turn is over.
    All of the balloons are numbered. The number under the balloon is how many points you will get by hitting it.
    You want as many points as possible!

Part 2: Car Race

    You want as many points as possible, because the amount of points you win is how many cm forward your car will move.
    The first car to reach the finish line is the winner of the game!
